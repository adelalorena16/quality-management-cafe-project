# Quality Management Cafe Project

This project was developed as a university team assignment for a fictional company called **A&A Cafe**.

## Project Overview
The project focused on applying quality management principles in a service business context. Together with a colleague, I worked on designing and documenting the company structure, operational processes, quality policy, and customer satisfaction tools.

## Main Topics Covered
- company description and objectives
- core, support, and management processes
- general process map
- quality policy
- documented procedure for coffee-based drink preparation
- customer satisfaction evaluation questionnaire

## Team Context
This was a team project developed together with a colleague from university for a fictional café business scenario.

## Skills Demonstrated
- process thinking
- quality management
- service operations design
- documentation
- organizational structuring
- customer-oriented improvement
